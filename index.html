Welcome to Hononegah Student Tech Help!

Who are we?

We are a group of student volunteers who learn about basic PC hardware through troubleshooting, documenting, and repairing Chromebooks from Hononegah and Rockton School District. Students have access to a workspace with tool kit, work lamps, chargers, an inventory of spare parts, and a friendly, clean, inclusive atmosphere.

Want to join?

Any interested student can visit the Tech Help room in the school library from 8-4 pm or email Daniel Burchardt at dburch@hononegah.org.
